Welcome to OpenBazaar

Stop and read this. Your money and privacy are at risk.
OpenBazaar is in beta. What this means for you:
  • You are not anonymous at all on the OpenBazaar network. Your IP is tied to contracts you sell, buy, or notarize. Malicious parties could use this information against you; protecting your privacy is your own responsibility.
  • Transactions may fail for technical reasons, and you can lose your bitcoins. In early beta we recommend smaller transactions and only using the bitcoin testnet. The OpenBazaar dev team is not responsible for any lost Bitcoin or product issues.
  • The software functionality will change over time. This means that you may lose your product listings or settings when we roll out new releases.
  • Proof-of-burn address format will change, and reputation pledges will be lost. Don't pledge too much on your stores yet!
  • There will be bugs. Submit a bug report in the forums or on Reddit, and please be patient.
